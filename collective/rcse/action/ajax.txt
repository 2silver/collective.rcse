Ajax Comments
=============

Commenting Process
------------------

* js: InitAjaxAction
  [resources/dev/js/themedesktop.js]
* js: UpdateComments
  [resources/dev/js/themedesktop.js]
* user: add a comment text
* user: click "comment" button
* js: post ajax request to @@plone.comments.ajax
  [resources/dev/js/themedesktop.js]
* server: process the form, block redirect and return content of document-actions-wrapper
  [collective.rcse.action.ajax.Comments]
